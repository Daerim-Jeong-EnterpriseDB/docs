---
title: Returning the Lasso report
---

The Lasso report on a single instance terminates
when the customer returns the following information
to their contact person at EDB:

1. the TAR file(s) from phase 1, by uploading it in the Portal
   ("Upload report" menu or as a specific ticket attachment)
2. a document containing the answers to the questions from phase 2,
   by uploading it in the Portal ("My Documents") section
